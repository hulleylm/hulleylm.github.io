AI for Terrain Generation

My Project

This is my final year project and the subject of my dissertation. The aim of this project is to discuss whether GANs can solve the problem of unreaistic terrain generated by more traditional fractal and noise techniques. Video game maps and terrain are often procedurally generated and can suffer the same drawbacks of unrealistic, repetitive terrain. I will be developing a GAN (Generative Adversarial Network) trained on satellite imagery and elevation data. This will form the basis of my discussion as a comparison against more traditional techniques.

GANs

First introduced by Ian Goodfellow et al. in 2014, GANs are a fairly recent development in Computer Science. The specific architecture I will be using for my model is StyleGAN, developed by NVIDIA.
