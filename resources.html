Automated Emails Using Google Apps Script

The Problem

In my year in industry, I worked as an intern on the Data, Tech, and Comms team in the careers department of the University of Liverpool. Each year it costs the department £8,000 to print resources booklets which are offered in the studio. These booklets often run out and the career coaches are told to only let people take them if necessary (due to the cost). My line manager asked if there was a way we could offer a digital version of the booklets but still have them as visible and accessible as they are in the studio.

My Solution

I was responsible for the tablets in the office and knew there were three, older samsung tablets thaat didn't get used as they were recently replaced with ipads. I wrote a Google Sheets script with an assosciated Google form where students could enter their email and recieve an email with useful links and their chosen resouces as pdf attachments. I presented this solution so i could put through a request for three lockable tablet wall mounts
